Thanksgiving

The time of year when you leave the ones you love
to be with your family.

// The Story

When a failed game designer is coerced home for the holidays, he discovers his reclusive father has built a quantum computer in the basement and has been secretly crossing into alternate dimensions — searching for the one thing none of his real family ever gave him. A reason to keep going.
